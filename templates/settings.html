{# ---- Kubernetes ---- #}
When enabled, the dashboard fetches Ingress hostnames from your cluster. The connection is auto-detected: in-cluster service account first, then /config/kubeconfig as a fallback. A read-only ClusterRole is sufficient — see the RBAC sample below.
Service account RBAC setup 
apiVersion: v1
kind: ServiceAccount
metadata:
  name: ddns-dashboard
  namespace: ddns
---
apiVersion: rbac.authorization.k8s.io/v1
kind: ClusterRole
metadata:
  name: ddns-dashboard-ingress-reader
rules:
  - apiGroups: ["networking.k8s.io"]
    resources: ["ingresses"]
    verbs: ["get", "list"]
---
apiVersion: rbac.authorization.k8s.io/v1
kind: ClusterRoleBinding
metadata:
  name: ddns-dashboard-ingress-reader
roleRef:
  apiGroup: rbac.authorization.k8s.io
  kind: ClusterRole
  name: ddns-dashboard-ingress-reader
subjects:
  - kind: ServiceAccount
    name: ddns-dashboard
    namespace: ddns
